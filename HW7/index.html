  • Prototype Closest Other Game: The game takes inspiration from incremental / idle clicker games like Cookie Clicker.
  • High Concept: From humble beginnings, become a master student of CS 325 by upgrading your learning skills and collecting academic achievements.
  • Mandated Variety: Mouse / Clickable buttons, Random, Joker / Achiever, Incremental game
  • Prototype Goal: This prototype is having fun by making a game themed after our class.
  • Player Experience Goals: Players should feel the thrill of battling dangerous enemies.
  • Gameplay: Click on the different "learning tasks" to "learn" them. Collect knowledge by clicking on the coins (or letting them be picked up after 3 seconds). Upgrade your learning skill and
  • Strategies: Try to strategically balance your money to purchase the different upgrades.
  • Story/Setting/Premise: You've just arrived for your first week of school at GMU. The class you're most excited about is CS 325--as a big fan of video games, you're looking to learn more about them in detail. Little did you know all of the rigorous academic trials you would face when you registered for the class. Upgrade your learning skills in order to become the best student there ever was!
  • Target Audience: The game primarily for students of CS 325.
  • Play Time: The game is never-ending, so it could be played as long as the player wants.
