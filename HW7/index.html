  • Prototype Closest Other Game: The game takes inspiration from games like Castlevania, Fire Emblem, Child of Light, and Pok√©mon.
  • High Concept: Escaping the crumbling castle, the player must overcome harrowing obstacles and defeat arcane enemies with their mystical bow and arrow.
  • Mandated Variety: Keyboard, Deterministic, Explorer / Achiever, RPG / Platformer / Adventure
  • Prototype Goal: This prototype is evaluating the design and function of the battle system.
  • Player Experience Goals: Players should feel the thrill of battling dangerous enemies.
  • Gameplay: On the overworld, the player walks around with the arrow keys. In the battle system, the player moves the menu to a move with the up and down arrow keys and selects it with the space key.
  • Strategies: Try to figure out which elements each enemy is weak to.
  • Story/Setting/Premise: You are an adventurer who has just unearthed a magical bow in an ancient castle. Upon grasping the relic, the bow releases a burst of energy that causes the castle to begin to collapse. What's more, it's awoken dangerous creatures that will hunt you with a vengeance! Can you escape the crumbling castle with your life?
  • Target Audience: The game is for fans of strategy games.
  • Play Time: 2-3 mins.
